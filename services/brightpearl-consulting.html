Introduction to our Brightpearl Automation and Reporting Service

Overview & Credentials

We have spent more than ten years inside Brightpearl. Our team built the custom Brightpearl Zapier integration with thirteen triggers and eighteen actions. We connect Brightpearl data to SyncHub so you can analyse it in tools like Power BI, Looker Studio and Tableau. Our Brightpearl Chrome Extension adds fast search, quick links and inline editing. When Brightpearl slows you down, we fix it.

Key Benefits

  • Save time on accounting tasks and day to day admin.
  • Cut data entry errors with automation and validation.
  • Push supplier bills and payment allocation straight through Brightpearl.
  • Gain real time insight with BI dashboards fed from SyncHub.

Brightpearl Chrome Extension
