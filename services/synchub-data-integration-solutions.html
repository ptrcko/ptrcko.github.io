Introduction to our SyncHub Data Integration for Franchisors Service

Synchub is a data integration tool that brings all of your SaaS data into one place.

SyncHub solves a critical challenge for franchisors managing multiple locations. Many franchisors operate separate instances of the same SaaS platforms for accounting (e.g., Xero), inventory management (e.g., Cin7), and point-of-sale systems (e.g., Lightspeed). This fragmentation makes aggregating financial, operational, and sales data across locations time-consuming and difficult. Without a centralised system, top-down reporting is slow, incomplete, and nearly impossible to automate. SyncHub eliminates these barriers by consolidating data from multiple sources into a single, structured repository, enabling real-time insights, seamless reporting, and better decision-making across the entire franchise network.

As an Australian Solutions Partner for SyncHub, ATOM Digital helps franchisors and multi-location businesses unlock the full potential of their data. Managing multiple franchise locations comes with data challenges, from inconsistent reporting across franchisees to difficulty in tracking overall business performance. Our expertise ensures you get the most out of SyncHub, streamlining your data management and giving you full control over how data is collected, shared, and utilised.

By working with ATOM Digital, you eliminate the complexity of data management. With expert guidance on multi-location data integration, you can focus on growth while having complete visibility over every franchise.
