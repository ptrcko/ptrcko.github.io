What is SFIA?

SFIA defines the skills and competencies required by professionals who design, develop, implement, manage and protect the data and technology that power the digital world.

It is a not-for-profit organisation that aims to set an international framework that can be used globally to define skills and competency for the digital world.

We use SFIA to give a clear indication of the level of skills and ownership that our different levels offer to our clients.

You can read more about SFIA here: https://sfia-online.org/en
